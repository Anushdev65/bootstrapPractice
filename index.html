Bendy Golden Leaf Tea

Morning Everyone! feel like trying the classic breakfast tea? Bendy Golden Leaf Tea- It is a classic mild black tea with the hint of coffee aroma, consisting a strong earthy flavour. Benefits of (Black Tea): - Rich in Antioxidant properties. - Helps with weight loss. - Boosts heart health. - Reduces LDL Cholesterol. - Helps to reduce high blood pressure. - May help to lower blood sugar levels. - Got properties to fight against cancer cells. - Helps to stay focused while it contains caffeine
Preparation Method: Better if you use filtered water. 2-3 grams of tea for a cup. 5-6 grams of Tea for a pot (500ml). Steeping Time: 2-5 minutes depends on how strong you want your tea to be. Water Temperature 75˚C, 85˚C (170˚F, 190˚F).
